What Is Project GOS

GOS is a story driven pixel art adventure game filled with spell slinging turn based strategy! Create Spells, Craft Relics, Unlock Powerful Characters, Cook Foods, Mix Potions, Interact with various NPCs and more!

Stylists

In GOS, you take control of a 'Stylist'; a person (or beast) capable of wielding powerful magic. Choose from a growing list of unlockable Stylists, each equipped with their own unique passive effect. Unique passives encourage unique playstyles!

Spells!

Create Spells. Pick and choose which spells to build with. Take your build and embark on quests! Each spell is unique; synergize spells together with your chosen Stylist's Passive for Powerful Results!

Battle System

Take your build to battle, challenge players(Locked) and enemies to defeat them and collect the items they drop! Despite being called a turn-based game, in Project GOS, both players will concurrently choose their attack within a 6 second window (Disabled in Solo mode). Choose between Rohkan(Rock), Paperious(Paper), and Scissora(Scissors), and dominate rounds to quickly defeat your opponents.
