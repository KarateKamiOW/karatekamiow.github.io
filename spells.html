Spells

Craft and Unlock various spells to expand your build options and find new options and creative ways to defeat your opponents!

In Gos, players must select 2 spells of each God Type(Rock-Paper-Scissors).

Synergize different spells with different Stylists for devastating effects!

Power Punch (Rock)

When you Style Win with 'Power Punch' Spell, you will gain bonus damage and punch twice instead of once!

Paper Soldier (Paper)

'Paper Soldier' will spawn a Paper Soldier ally on your side of the field. It will strike your opponent each round!

Venom Blade(Scissors)

'Venom Blade' will strike your opponent, dealing damage and inflicting a debilitating toxic status effect!
