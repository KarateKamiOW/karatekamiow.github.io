The Battle System

Simple to understand, Challenging to Master!

Fast Paced and Simple

In GOS, the draw of the game is collecting, flashy effects, and a simple yet challenging battle system to master. The focus on GOS' Battle System is to keep players engaged at all points in the game, while keeping it easy to understand for both the player and viewers!

Win The Round

Each round, both players will choose between either casting a spell of a specific God Type(Rock-Paper-Scissors) or Block (2 turn Cooldown). Each spell comes equipped with either a SW (Style Win) Bonus, a BB (Block Break) Bonus, or both. To SW, cast a spell with a SW Bonus against its weaker God Type (Rock -> Scissors, Paper -> Rock, Scissors -> Paper). To BB, cast a spell with a BB Bonus against a Blocking Opponent.

CLANG!

When you and your opponent both cast a spell of the same God Type, neither spell will cast, and instead both players will earn a 'CLANG' stack. The first player to SW/BB will use their 'CLANG' stacks, gaining 5* Bonus Damage for each stack!

Block!

See a big attack coming? Time your blocks to avoid ALL incoming damage that round! Blocking has a 2 turn cooldown, and will leave you defenseless if the opponent uses a Block Break Spell!

Summon Allies

Summon allies to help dish out damage or change the flow of a given battle. Allies have their own health and unique effects, and can only be damaged by spells that can reach them! Players can have up to 3 active allies at a time.
