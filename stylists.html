Stylists

In GOS, you collect and unlock special magic imbued characters called Stylists. Each Stylist has their own unique Passive Effect, incentivizing different build paths for each!

Each Stylist offers a different play experience and play pattern. As more spells are released, players should have the freedom to build any given Stylist in a number of ways!

Ridge

When you Style Win or Block Break with a Scissora(Scissors) Spell, Ridge will additionally swing his blade and slice his opponent, dealing bonus damage and bleeding!

Fia

When you successfully block, Fia will warp into her portal before reemerging, healing health and gaining bonus damage!

Awgol

Every few rounds, a 'meat' ally will spawn on your side of the field. When you Style Win or Block Break with one active, Awgol will eat it, healing health, permanently gaining armor per round, and permanently increasing his damage for the game!
