Omari W.

Hello. My name is Omari W. I am a 25 year old Computer Scientist, Pixel Artist, Pixel Animator, Online Programming/Game/Animator Tutor, music producer, Fx Artist.. A little bit of everything really.

I have dreams of one day changing the world by bringing people across the globe together to play my games!

Around when Covid first hit I decided to take up making games, something I wanted to do for a long time. For the first year and a half, I learned quite a lot, and of course created a few duds.

However, starting in September of 2021, I decided to begin on a project I knew I'd see through, Project GOS. Since then, I've been programming, Pixel Drawing, or just coming up with new game designs nearly every day.

Although I enjoy game designing my own personal project, I believe I'm ready to take my skills to the next step and apply what I know to a business.

Thank you for taking the time to view my single project Portfolio. In the mean time, how about you try out my demo? :)

Unity

Project GOS is created using Unity and C#. Over the past few years I've taught myself C#. I like this language.

Pixel Art

Project GOS is created using Pixel Art. A fast and relatively easy to learn art medium. It helps that I've grown up on Pixel games, and as a result, naturally love making them.
