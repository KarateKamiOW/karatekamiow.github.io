Extras!

There's more to GOS than just battling!

Turn the items you collect into Foods, Potions, and even Relics, all of which will assist you greatly on your adventure!

Cooking

The best way to earn money(Solcs) around these parts is to beat enemies up, collect the drops, and mix them together into a delicious meal! The more you cook, the higher your cooking rank will be (Unranked-D-C-B-A-S). The higher your rank, the more recipes you'll be able to cook! Sell the food you cook to earn Solcs FAST!

Potion Mixing

As enemies become stronger (Solo Mode), the only way to even the power difference is to create and consume potions for temporary boosts. Donate items to the local towns Potion Mixery to unlock Potion Recipes, mix powerful potions and consume different potions to stack temporary buffs! After creating enough potions, the Mixery will be able to sell you that potion type directly instead of needing to mix the again!

Relic Crafting

Relics are powerful equip-able pieces that offer your Stylist a secondary Passive Effect! Players can decide which Relic to choose from, further pushing building creativity! Like cooking, collect the items needed to craft a Relic. Once created, you can 'consume' the Relic to permanently unlock and use forever!
